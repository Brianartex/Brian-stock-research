🐂 Bull Case

The AI Agent wave is DocuSign's next wave. AI agents need to execute agreements. DocuSign has 1.5 billion+ agreements in its network — the largest structured agreement dataset in the world.

🐻 Bear Case

AI agents could disintermediate DocuSign entirely. If Microsoft, Google, or OpenAI build agreement execution directly into their AI platforms, DocuSign's moat shrinks considerably.

My Call: HOLD. The AI tailwind is real but not yet priced in. Wait for evidence of re-acceleration before building a full position. Current entry around $48 offers reasonable risk/reward for a starter position.
