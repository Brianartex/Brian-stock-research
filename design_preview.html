Bull Case

The AI Agent wave is DocuSign's next wave. AI agents need to execute agreements. DocuSign has 1.5 billion+ agreements in its network — the largest structured agreement dataset in the world. Its AI Agent Studio lets customers build agents that negotiate, execute, and manage agreements automatically. Management guides for AI-driven automation becoming a meaningful contributor in 2026–2027.

Bear Case

AI agents could disintermediate DocuSign entirely. If Microsoft, Google, or OpenAI build agreement execution directly into their AI platforms, DocuSign's moat shrinks considerably. The company's growth has decelerated from 40%+ to single digits over three years — the structural growth story is in question.

My Call: HOLD. The AI tailwind is real but not yet priced in. Wait for evidence of re-acceleration before building a full position. Current entry around $48 offers reasonable risk/reward for a starter position. Catalyst: Q2 FY2027 earnings showing AI Agent Studio revenue contribution.
