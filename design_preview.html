◈ Bull Case

The AI Agent wave is DocuSign's next act. AI agents need to execute agreements. DocuSign has 1.5 billion+ agreements — the largest structured agreement dataset in the world. AI Agent Studio lets customers build agents that negotiate and execute autonomously.

Valuation is pricing in collapse. ~6x forward EV/revenue for a company still growing. If AI adoption drives re-acceleration to 12–15%, the stock deserves 10–12x. Risk/reward asymmetrically favors buyers at these levels.

◈ Bear Case

AI could disintermediate DocuSign entirely. If Microsoft, Google, or OpenAI build agreement execution into their platforms, DocuSign's moat shrinks. Growth decelerated from 40%+ to single digits over three years.

The turnaround isn't confirmed. One quarter of stabilization doesn't make a trend. Billings growth remains weak, and the enterprise segment is seeing increased competitive pressure from Adobe and Dropbox.

My Call · Hold

The AI tailwind is real but not yet priced in. Wait for evidence of re-acceleration before building a full position. Current entry around $48 offers reasonable risk/reward for a starter position. Catalyst: Q2 FY2027 showing AI Agent Studio revenue contribution above 5% of total.
