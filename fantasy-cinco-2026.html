Keeper Strategy — How to Decide
Keep If...
  • Player is projected top-5 at their position and keeper cost is below market ADP
  • Player was drafted for $1-5 and is now a starter (free money)
  • You have a top-3 QB in superflex — the positional scarcity is real
  • Rookie RBs who broke out (Jeanty $53 keep, Hampton $30 keep)
Don't Keep If...
  • Keeper cost exceeds projected auction value (you're overpaying)
  • Player is aging out (McCaffrey at $53 is risky at his age/injury history)
  • You can get similar production for less in the draft
  • You're keeping a player just because you like them — be ruthless
